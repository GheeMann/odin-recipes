Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of the finest red wine grapes
  • A blend of herbs and spices (1 tablespoon each of thyme and rosemary, a pinch of celestial cinnamon)
  • 1 ounce of the wisdom of Odin (brewed essence or infused elixir)
  • Aged in oak barrels for 12 months
  • Enchanted fermentation process with celestial energy
  • Filtered through Odin's wisdom, precisely 2 purification cycles
  • Bottled with reverence in 750ml containers

Step 1: Infusion and Aging

  1. Select 2 pounds of the finest red wine grapes.
  2. Mix a tablespoon each of thyme and rosemary. Add a pinch of celestial cinnamon.
  3. Incorporate 1 ounce of Odin's wisdom, either in brewed essence or infused elixir.
  4. Transfer the grape and spice blend to oak barrels for 12 months, letting it absorb the nuances of the wood.

Step 2: Purification and Bottling

  • Initiate an enchanted fermentation process infused with celestial energy.
  • Filter the elixir through Odin's wisdom, undergoing precisely 2 purification cycles for exceptional clarity.
  • Transfer the elixir with reverence into 750ml containers, sealing each with the magic of Odin's touch.
  • Pour a glass of God Wine, savoring the celestial symphony of flavors in every sip. Commune with the generosity of Odin in this extraordinary elixir.
