Kelvin Gelato

A Chilled Symphony of Celestial Indulgence

Embark on a taste journey fit for the gods with Kelvin Gelato by Odin, a frozen masterpiece that transcends the ordinary. This celestial creation begins with a velvety base crafted from a blend of whole milk and luxurious heavy cream, forming the canvas for an indulgence guided by divine sweetness. Enhanced with a dash of Odin's celestial vanilla extract, the gelato base becomes a harmonious blend of creamy richness and celestial flavor, promising a treat that's both ethereal and deeply satisfying.

As the gelato churns to perfection, the kitchen is filled with the irresistible aroma of vanilla and the promise of a celestial symphony in each scoop. With each scoop, savor the extraordinary combination of heavenly sweetness and creamy perfection, as Odin's gelato becomes a testament to the divine artistry of frozen treats.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • A dash of Odin's celestial vanilla extract
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • A sprinkle of stardust (optional for celestial ambiance)

Step 1: Gelato Base Creation

  1. Heat 2 cups of whole milk and 1 cup of heavy cream in a saucepan until it simmers. Remove from heat.
  2. Whisk together 3/4 cup granulated sugar and 4 large egg yolks until fluffy. Slowly pour the warm milk mixture into the egg mixture, then return it to the saucepan.
  3. Add a dash of Odin's celestial vanilla extract and stir gently to infuse the gelato base with divine flavor.
  4. Cook the mixture over low heat until it thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon. Allow it to cool and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.

Step 2: Churning and Freezing Perfection

  • Pour the chilled mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  • For celestial ambiance, sprinkle stardust over the churned gelato.
  • Transfer the churned gelato into a lidded container and freeze until reaching the desired consistency.
  • Scoop Odin's Kelvin Gelato into bowls or cones, savoring each bite of this divine treat that transcends the ordinary.
